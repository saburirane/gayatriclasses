About Us

We offer coaching for a range of academic programs from KG to PG under one roof, including 12th Commerce/Science, 10th SSC & CBSE, T.Y.B.Com, SYBCom & FYBCom. Our qualified and experienced teaching staff provides personalized attention and ensures timely completion of the syllabus. At Gayatri Classes, we believe in providing an affordable fee structure without compromising on the quality of education. We also offer study materials, exclusive weekly tests, and regular feedback to parents. In addition, we now offer online lectures for students who prefer to learn from the comfort of their own homes.

My Services

Online lectures

As mentioned earlier, Gayatri Classes now offers online lectures for students who prefer to learn from the comfort of their own homes. This service allows students to access our high-quality education from anywhere in the world with an internet connection.
